# Welcome

**The first on-chain ratio perpetuals exchange.**

Structured Perps is a decentralized exchange on Hyperliquid for ratio perpetual contracts. Trade the relative performance between two assets — like HYPE/ETH or HYPE/SOL — in a single position, with no counterparty risk and full self-custody.

## Why ratio perps?

A ratio perp tracks `price(A) / price(B)`. Instead of managing two separate positions to pair trade, you get:

* **Single position** — One margin, one liquidation price, one fee
* **No liquidation trap** — Liquidation is based on the ratio, not individual asset prices. You can't get liquidated when the ratio moves in your favor.
* **Up to 10x leverage** — Capital-efficient exposure to relative value
* **24/7 trading** — Crypto-native assets, markets never close

## Markets

| Market | Ratio | Description |
|--------|-------|-------------|
| **HYPE/ETH** | ~0.0088 | Hyperliquid vs Ethereum |
| **HYPE/SOL** | ~0.12 | Hyperliquid vs Solana |
| **HYPE/BNB** | ~0.028 | Hyperliquid vs BNB Chain |

All markets are HIP-3 perpetuals deployed on Hyperliquid with hourly funding.

## Yield

Stake HYPE in the spHYPE vault and earn from two real revenue sources:

* **Validator staking rewards** — Native Hyperliquid staking yield
* **Trading fee share** — A portion of all trading fees generated across our markets

Early participants may be rewarded beyond standard yield.
