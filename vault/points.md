# Points Program

## Overview

The Structured Perps points program rewards early participants who stake HYPE and trade on the platform. Points are complementary to yield — they represent a commitment to early supporters.

## Earning points

### Staking

Depositors earn points proportional to their svHYPE holdings over time. A tier system rewards larger and longer-term stakers:

| Tier | Requirement | Multiplier |
|------|-------------|------------|
| Bronze | 100+ HYPE | 1.0x |
| Silver | 1,000+ HYPE | 1.2x |
| Gold | 5,000+ HYPE | 1.5x |
| Platinum | 10,000+ HYPE | 2.0x |
| Diamond | 25,000+ HYPE | 2.5x |
| Master | 50,000+ HYPE | 3.0x |
| Legend | 100,000+ HYPE | 5.0x |

### Trading

Active traders earn points proportional to their trading volume across all ratio perp markets.

## Withdrawal impact

Points use a **LIFO (Last In, First Out)** model. If you withdraw, the most recently earned points are forfeited first. This incentivizes long-term commitment and discourages farm-and-dump behavior.

## Emission schedule

* Points are emitted weekly
* **First week: 5x boosted emission** to reward the earliest participants
* Weekly emissions are fixed thereafter

## Seasons

The program operates in seasons. Season 1 begins at launch. Future seasons may introduce new earning mechanisms and partner integrations.
